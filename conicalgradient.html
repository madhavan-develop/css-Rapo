Neymar

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992), known as Neymar Júnior or mononymously as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team. The most expensive player ever, he is a prolific goalscorer and renowned playmaker widely regarded as one of the best players in the world and one of the greatest Brazilian players of all time.[3][4][5] Neymar has scored at least 100 goals for three different clubs, making him one of four players to achieve this feat.
